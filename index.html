Eat to Win

Revitalize your body before exercise with a foundation of nutrient-rich foods. Incorporate bananas for quick energy, oatmeal for sustained fuel, whole grain bread paired with peanut butter for a balanced blend, Greek yogurt for protein and probiotics, eggs for complete protein, and protein smoothies for a convenient nutrient boost.

Before Exercise

Prime your body for peak performance with these pre-workout powerhouses. Bananas offer quick energy, oatmeal provides sustained fuel, while whole grain bread paired with peanut butter delivers a balanced blend of carbs and protein. Greek yogurt is a protein-packed option, eggs provide complete protein, and protein smoothies offer a convenient nutrient boost.

During Exercise

Sustain your energy levels during exercise with strategic fueling. Opt for sports drinks to replenish electrolytes, energy gels for quick carbohydrate boosts, and fruit for natural sugars and hydration.

After Exercise

Rebuild and repair your muscles with a post-workout focus on protein and carbohydrates. Incorporate lean proteins like chicken and fish, along with protein shakes for a quick recovery boost. Include complex carbohydrates from sweet potatoes, quinoa, and brown rice to replenish glycogen stores. Add healthy fats from avocado, nuts, seeds, and olive oil for overall well-being and nutrient absorption.
